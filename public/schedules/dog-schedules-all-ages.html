Newborn (0–8 weeks)

This stage is entirely mom-led. Puppies are blind, deaf, and fully dependent. Your role is monitoring health, weight, and warmth — not scheduling. They nurse every 2 hours around the clock.

🐣 Key Notes If mom is present, let her do the work — your job is observation. Call your vet immediately if any pup is losing weight, crying constantly, rejected by mom, or feels cold to the touch. The socialization window (weeks 3–12) is the most critical period for a dog's entire life — exposure now shapes temperament forever.
